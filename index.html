  • Implanted Online Attendence Management System

    1. Accuracy Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with suppoting computational devices. Automated attendence management systems ensure accurate time records and minimize the inevitable and costly errors with manual data entry. This accurate data thereby helps to provide accurate performace and payroll data.
    2. Economics Have you ever thought tht attendance management systems could control costs? It saves money by putting an end to inacurate time reporting buddy punching, absenteeism, tardiness, time abuse, and overpayment. As a matter of fact, a study done by the Axsium Group found that large organizations save up to $1,600 per employee after implementing automated attendance management systems.

It takes time to process paper sheets and time cards create payroll manually. Free up precious administration time with and automated system that does it all for you from keeping tracking of employees hours to automatically importing information into your payroll system. The time and effort saved combined with data accuracy helps in optimising the use of resources which lead to increased productivity and improved profits.
